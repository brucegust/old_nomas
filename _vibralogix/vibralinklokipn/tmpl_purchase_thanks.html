Obtaining CEU Credit:
If you wish to receive Continuing Education Units (CEUs) you must complete a 10-question True/False quiz for each Series One course ordered (or a single, 11 question quiz if you have ordered the 11-talk package). For Series Two courses (Symposia), a True/False quiz of five questions will be presented for each course ordered. Series Three is broken up into two separate courses with a quiz at the end of each one. Series Four has a ten question quiz at the conclusion of the sixth session.

To obtain CEUs you must score 100% correct on each quiz. You will have two opportunities to take each quiz. If necessary, you will be able to review the video(s) after taking each quiz the first time, in preparation for a second attempt. After the second attempt, the quiz will no longer be available.

Once you have successfully completed the course(s), your certificate of completion will be mailed to you at the address provided. If you have questions or problems along the way please contact us through the website.
